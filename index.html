ROADMAP

Our roadmap outlines the development and rollout of the Volamena tokenization platform and mining operations:

Phase 1

Preparation and Legal Compliance (Q1) Secure legal and regulatory approvals for tokenization of mining assets. Valuation of the mining assets and mineral reserves in the Tsaratanana region. Develop the Volamena blockchain platform, ensuring compliance with global KYC/AML standards.

Phase 2

Token Development and Platform Launch (Q2) Develop and test the Mining Operations Token (MOT) and Mineral Byproduct Tokens (MBT) on the blockchain. Establish smart contract functionality for profit-sharing and governance. Launch the Volamena platform for early investors.

Phase 3

Initial Token Offering (ITO) (Q3) Conduct the Initial Token Offering (ITO) for the Mining Operations Token (MOT). Onboard institutional and retail investors through a global marketing campaign. Secure listings on decentralized exchanges for token liquidity.

Phase 4

Mining Operations and Production (Q4) Expand mining operations in Tsaratanana with proceeds from the ITO. Start the first phase of mineral extraction and production (gold, beryl, amethyst, quartz, and gypsum). Distribute the first round of profits to token holders via smart contracts.

Phase 5

Platform Expansion and Ecosystem Growth (Q1 - Q2, Next Year). Expand the platform to include additional mining projects. Introduce secondary services like staking and lending based on tokenized mineral assets. Launch a marketplace for the direct trade of mineral byproduct tokens (MBT).

The Volamena Token Ecosystem

Mining Operations Token

(MOT)
Type:

ERC-20 or equivalent (Utility and Governance)

Supply:

Limited to represent the entire mining operation for the duration of the permits (renewable every 8 years).

Utility:

Ownership in the mining infrastructure, voting rights on key decisions, and a share in mining profits.

Profit Distribution:

Token holders receive revenue based on gold and mineral sales.

Mineral Byproduct Tokens

(MBT)
Type:

ERC-721 or ERC-1155 NFTs (Mineral-backed tokens)

Supply:

Each token represents a specific quantity of a mined mineral (e.g., 1 token = 1 gram of gold or its equivalent in other minerals).

Utility:

Ownership of specific mineral production, redeemable for physical delivery or tradeable on secondary markets.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Join Volamena Today and Be a Part of the Future of Mining Sign up now and take your first step towards owning a piece of a real gold mine.

What does tokenizing mining mean?
Tokenization involves converting ownership of real mining assets into digital tokens that are secured on the blockchain. These tokens give holders a stake in the mining operation and allow them to profit from the sale of gold and other minerals.
How do I earn returns?
As the mine produces gold and minerals, a portion of the sales is distributed to token holders. The more tokens you own, the higher your share of the profits.
Is this a secure investment?
Gold and minerals have always been seen as stable, long-term stores of value. By leveraging blockchain technology, we ensure that your investment is transparent, traceable, and secure.
How is this different from buying gold?
Unlike traditional gold investment, where you need to store and safeguard physical gold, Volamena tokens are digital and liquid, allowing for easier trading and ownership. Plus, you earn regular profits from mining operations, making it more dynamic than simply holding gold.
Can I trade or sell my tokens?
Yes, Volamena tokens are tradeable on decentralized exchanges, giving you the flexibility to buy, sell, or hold them as you see fit.
